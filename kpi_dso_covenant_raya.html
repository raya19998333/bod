Key Observations
DSO — Collection Risk
DSO stands at 101 days, 57% above budget (64 days) and rising from 96 days in Dec-2025. AR of OMR 56,107K has grown faster than revenue, signalling a collection lag that requires management intervention.
Covenant Formula
Covenant = EBITDA (Mar-26 + Mar-25) − VOWD. With EBITDA Mar-26 at OMR 13,153K and VOWD at OMR 8,756K, the partial result is OMR 4,397K. EBITDA Mar-25 is unavailable in source data (formula error in FS Row 24).
Covenant Compliance
Both financial covenants are met: DSCR at 161.23x (min 1.10x) and Net Debt/Equity at −0.08x (max 2.33x). The company is in a net cash position, with cash and deposits exceeding total indebtedness by OMR 16,933K.
